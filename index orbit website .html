Built from a real problem

Orbit began when long workweeks away from home made it too easy for schedules, errands, and small household details to disappear between conversations, texts, and separate apps.

The weekly hook

Start with the moments that already happen.

Trust is a product feature

Your household should not be the product.

Orbit’s public version is being designed around account-level security, household isolation, private storage, data export, deletion controls, and tightly scoped AI access.

01

Household isolationMembers only access the household they belong to.

02

Private by defaultNo public files, open databases, or advertising profiles.

03

AI with boundariesJuno receives only the minimum context required.

04

User controlExport, disconnect, and delete your data.
